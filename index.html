Section 1

Introduction

Welcome to AssetFlow ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the AssetFlow mobile application ("App"), available on the Apple App Store.

By downloading, accessing, or using AssetFlow, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

Section 2

Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you create an account or use our App, we collect the following information:

  • Company name and email address
  • Account credentials (passwords are stored as secure, one-way hashes and are never stored in plain text)
  • Asset information (names, descriptions, unique IDs, notes)
  • Category names and icons
  • Team member names and departments
  • Asset check-in/check-out history and associated photos
  • Subscription and billing preferences

2.2 Information from Third-Party Sign-In Services

If you sign in using Apple Sign-In, we receive:

  • Your Apple ID user identifier
  • Your name (if you choose to share it)
  • Your email address (or an Apple-provided private relay email if you choose to hide your email)

We do not receive your Apple ID password. Apple Sign-In is processed in accordance with Apple's privacy policies.

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we may automatically collect:

  • Device type, model, and operating system version
  • App usage data and interaction patterns
  • Error logs and crash reports
  • Subscription status and plan information

2.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect precise location data
  • We do not collect contacts or address book data
  • We do not use device advertising identifiers
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites
Section 3

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the App
  • To create and manage your account
  • To process asset tracking operations (check-in, check-out, history)
  • To manage your subscription plan and process plan changes
  • To send you service-related notifications (e.g., subscription renewals, account alerts)
  • To respond to your requests and support inquiries
  • To monitor and analyze usage trends to improve the App
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security vulnerabilities
  • To enforce our Terms and Conditions

We process your information based on our legitimate business interest in providing and improving the App, and based on your consent when you create an account.

Section 4

In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

AssetFlow offers auto-renewable subscription plans (Pro and Business) through the Apple App Store. When you subscribe:

  • All payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information, credit card numbers, or Apple Pay details.
  • Subscription management, including renewals, cancellations, and billing, is handled entirely by Apple.
  • You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID account settings (Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions).
  • We receive confirmation of your subscription status from Apple to unlock the appropriate features in the App.

For more information on how Apple handles your payment data, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Section 5

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a hosted backend platform that provides enterprise-grade security measures, including:

  • Encryption of data at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+)
  • Row-level security policies ensuring data isolation between accounts
  • Regular automated backups
  • SOC 2 Type II compliant infrastructure

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include secure password hashing, authenticated API endpoints, and role-based access controls.

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Section 6

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not share your data with advertisers.

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We use Supabase for data storage and backend services. Supabase processes your data on our behalf under strict confidentiality obligations and data processing agreements.
  • Apple: Apple processes your subscription payments and provides us with subscription status information. Apple operates under its own Privacy Policy.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, court order, or governmental regulation, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction. We will notify you of any such change.
  • With Your Consent: We may share your information with third parties when you have given us explicit consent to do so.
Section 7

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. Specifically:

  • Account data is retained for the duration of your account.
  • Asset history is retained based on your subscription plan (limited days on Free plan, extended or unlimited on paid plans).
  • If you delete your account, all associated data — including assets, categories, members, history, photos, and profile information — will be permanently and irreversibly deleted from our systems within 30 days.

We may retain anonymized, aggregated data that cannot identify you for analytical purposes.

Section 8

Your Rights

Regardless of your location, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can access all personal information stored in the App at any time through the App interface.
  • Correction: You can update or correct your information directly within the App.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through Profile > Account Settings > Delete Account.
  • Data Portability: You can export your data in CSV format (available on Pro and Business plans).
  • Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw consent at any time by discontinuing use of the App and deleting your account.
Section 9

Additional Rights for California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know: You have the right to request that we disclose the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.
  • Right to Delete: You have the right to request deletion of your personal information.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights.
  • No Sale of Data: We do not sell your personal information and have not sold personal information in the preceding 12 months.

To exercise your CCPA rights, please contact us at contactassetflow@gmail.com.

Section 10

Additional Rights for EU/EEA Residents (GDPR)

If you are a resident of the European Union or European Economic Area, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Legal Basis: We process your data based on your consent (account creation) and our legitimate interest in providing and improving the service.
  • Right to Rectification: You may request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to Erasure: You may request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You may request that we restrict the processing of your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You may request a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: You may object to the processing of your personal data.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority in your country of residence.

To exercise your GDPR rights, please contact us at contactassetflow@gmail.com.

Section 11

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. Supabase infrastructure may store data in data centers located in the United States and other regions. Where data is transferred outside your jurisdiction, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

Section 12

Children's Privacy

AssetFlow is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in the EU/EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal data from a child under the applicable age, we will take immediate steps to delete such information from our systems.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at contactassetflow@gmail.com.

Section 13

Photos & Camera Access

The App may request access to your device's camera and photo library for the purpose of attaching photos to asset check-in and check-out records. Specifically:

  • Camera access is used solely for capturing asset photos within the App.
  • Photo library access is used solely for selecting existing photos to attach to asset records.
  • Photos are uploaded securely and stored in your account's private storage. They are not shared with other users or third parties.
  • Camera and photo library access is entirely optional. You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device's Settings > Privacy & Security.
Section 14

Tracking & Advertising

AssetFlow does not track you across other companies' apps or websites. We do not use the Apple Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA). We do not serve advertisements within the App. We do not share any data with advertising networks or data brokers.

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Displaying a notice within the App for significant changes

Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
